Andriy Shevchenko

Andrey Nikolaevich Shevchenko is a Ukrainian football coach and politician who previously played as a striker. Honored Master of Sports of Ukraine

Later, a football coach. From 2016 to 2021, he was the head coach of the national team of Ukraine. From November 2021 to January 2022, he headed the coaching staff of the Italian "Genoa". In 2003, he became the first Ukrainian to win the Champions League. The winner of the "Golden Ball" 2004 is a prize awarded to the best football player in the world (at that time - the best football player in Europe). One of the most effective scorers of European cup tournaments for all seasons. The best scorer of the national team of Ukraine. He became the top scorer of the Champions League three times, and the Italian championship twice. The second scorer in the entire history of "Milan". A legend of Ukraine and an example for young people. Now it helps Ukraine a lot on the information front.
